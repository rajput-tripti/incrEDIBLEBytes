About Team IncrEdibleBytes

IncreEDIBLE Bytes is a platform which will end the hunger related problems. This platform is developed as a result of Call For Code 2021, WiT Hackathon by a team of technologists who are using tech in solving social problems. This platform uses Angular 12 as web tech and IBM Cloud as serverless APIs. Feel free to checkout the team members who gave their best in developing this platform.
